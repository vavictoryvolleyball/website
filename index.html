Growing homeschool volleyball across Virginia

Building more opportunities for homeschool athletes to compete, grow, and lead.

Virginia Victory Volleyball exists to strengthen homeschool volleyball in Virginia through competitive teams, positive coaching, and a clear pathway for athletes and families. With the support of 540 Volleyball Academy, we are working to create a stronger future for the game.

Explore Programs Support the Growth
Who We Are

A nonprofit volleyball program focused on homeschool athletes and family-centered growth.

Virginia Victory Volleyball was created to help meet a growing need in Virginia: more high-quality volleyball opportunities for homeschool athletes. Our goal is to give players a place to train, compete, belong, and develop as leaders while helping families find a strong and positive athletic community.

Opportunity

We are expanding access to competitive volleyball for homeschool athletes who want to keep growing.

Development

We focus on skill, confidence, discipline, teamwork, and leadership at every level.

Community

We are building a program where athletes, parents, coaches, and partners move in the same direction.

Our Focus

Helping homeschool volleyball grow beyond one team, one season, or one gym.

Homeschool volleyball in Virginia is growing, and that growth needs structure, support, and a clear vision. Virginia Victory Volleyball is committed to helping athletes develop from middle school through high school while creating a program that can serve families year after year.

Programs

A pathway for athletes at every stage.

01

Middle School / 14U

A development-focused entry point where younger athletes learn the game, build confidence, and prepare for higher levels of competition.

02

High School Volleyball

Competitive teams for homeschool athletes ready to train with purpose, represent the program, and pursue excellence together.

03

Travel Teams

Competing under the 540 Volleyball Academy umbrella, our travel teams provide homeschool athletes with high-level coaching, competitive tournament experiences, and a clear pathway for long-term development.

04

Tournaments & Events

Competitive experiences that bring homeschool athletes, families, and programs together while raising the level of play in Virginia.

Supported by a strong local volleyball partner

Powered by 540 Volleyball Academy

Virginia Victory Volleyball is proud to work with 540 Volleyball Academy as a key partner in training, facilities, and program growth. Their support helps provide athletes with a serious volleyball environment while giving homeschool families a stronger foundation for long-term success.

Why It Matters

Growth requires more than registration numbers. It requires a culture.

We want homeschool volleyball in Virginia to be known for strong coaching, disciplined teams, supportive families, and athletes who compete with confidence. Our program is built to help players grow on the court while learning habits that carry into school, work, family, and life.
