Who We Are

A volleyball home built around purpose, effort, and opportunity.

Virginia Victory Volleyball is a nonprofit program focused on developing athletes through competitive volleyball, positive coaching, leadership development, and a team-first culture. We believe athletes grow best when they are challenged, supported, and surrounded by people who care about who they are becoming.

Compete

We prepare athletes to play with confidence, discipline, and toughness.

Lead

We teach players to communicate, serve others, and take ownership.

Belong

We build a culture where athletes and families feel seen, supported, and valued.

Our Mission

More than a season. More than a scoreboard.

Our mission is to provide competitive volleyball opportunities that help homeschool athletes develop skill, character, confidence, and leadership while building a positive community for families.
